The Story of Notey Bear

Deep in the pixelated forest Notey the bear found a glowing coin under a honey tree. Curious, he tapped it and coins burst into the air like golden pollen. Soon, animals from all over joined in, tapping and laughing together. The forest economy bloomed with fun and friendship at its core. Notey decided to share the joy by creating his own coin Notey Coin. Now, from forest to city, people trade Notey for laughs, memories, and sometimes snacks. And every tap brings the world a little closer to happiness.
