FAQ

It says “Template not found”.

The link may be private or expired. Ask the creator for a fresh link or try again later.

The app didn’t open on my phone.

Tap Open in CapCut app. If it still doesn’t open, update CapCut from the App Store / Play Store and try again.

Can I change the music?

Yes—swap music in CapCut and re-align the beat markers. Make sure you have rights to use the track.
